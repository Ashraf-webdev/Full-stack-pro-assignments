2. What are HTML Entities? List out 5 commonly used HTML entities.


Ans: HTML entities are special codes or sequences of characters used in HTML to represent characters and symbols that 
     may not be easily typed or displayed directly in your web page's code.
     
     Here are 5 commonly used HTML entities-
       a. &amp; - Exclamation mark - &#33
       b. &amp; - Ampersand - &#38
       c. &lt; - Less than sign - &#60
       d. &gt; - Greater than sign - &#62
       e. &dollar; - Dollar sign - &#36
